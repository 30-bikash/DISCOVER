WHY BORA BORA ?

Jewel of the South Seas

Undoubtedly the most celebrated island in the South Pacific, Bora Bora is
French Polynesia's leading lady.Her beauty is unrivaled and her fame, unwavering.
Bora Bora is one of the few places on earth that everyone hopes to witness in their
lifetime—and once you see it, you are forever enamored.

The Locale

Where is Bora Bora, exactly?The island lies just northwest of Tahiti, less than an
hour away by plane from Papeete. The airport is located on a small,separate part of the
island known as Motu Mete. Upon arrival, you will be greeted with a warm smile
and fragrant flower lei and transferred by boat to your Bora Bora resort, meaning
you hardly have to wait for your first encounter with the island's famously
translucent lagoon. The view from the plane window is a moment you will not soon forget.
Have your camera in hand as you begin your descent and prepare for the moment
when iconic Mount Otemanu comes into view. From that point on, continue to exceed even your highest expectations.

Final Impression

Once seen, Bora Bora is never forgotten. From the day you arrive until the moment
you part ways, you will be in awe of how her soaring mountain peaks, turquoise
lagoon and luxuriant overwater bungalows somehow look even more spectacular
than the pictures.

ACTIVITIES

LAND TOUR

See Bora Bora by both land! This tour combines the best of world so you can experience all the highlights of the island, even if you’re short on time.

TOUR WITH ECO SHARKS AND STINGRAYS

Swim with friendly sharks, stingrays, and vibrant tropical fish before enjoying dinner with your feet in the lagoon on a beautiful private Polynesian island.

PAUL GAUGUIN CRUISES

Designed specifically for Polynesia, the Paul Gauguin is a deluxe vessel offering all-inclusive small ship luxury to just 332 guests. Flawless, friendly service from a European-trained staff emulates the delightfully unhurried and unregimented Polynesian way of life.

BORA BORA STARGAZING TOUR

Take a romantic sunset boat cruise and enjoy the sights as well as an interesting stargazing narrative and legends of Polynesian sailors. This stargazing cruise is unforgettable!
